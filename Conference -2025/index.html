Welcome to ICRTACM-2025

ICRTACM-2025, an international conference focusing on intention of delivering outstanding outcomes and challenges in the field of Mathematical Science.

It intends to bring collectively researchers, scientists, technicians, and scholars in all fields of the applied sciences, healthcare and life sciences, for the propagation of original research conclusions, new approaches and developmental practices that yield real results. It is planned as a major event to be held at Bangalore (Karnataka), India during October 09-10, 2025 with the participation of a large number of active researchers from all over the world.

This conference will provide the premier multidisciplinary forum for researchers, practitioners and scientists to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends and practical challenges associated with field of Physical, Chemical, Mathematical, Biological, and Health Science.
